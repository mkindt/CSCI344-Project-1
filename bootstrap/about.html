About TweetBeat

New to the woods, wondering what's happening and what is really popular? Let down by the events websites that seem uninspired? Sick of trying to sort through reviews of closed-down restaurants? Or just curious to know about interests in your area outside your normal scope....

TweetBeat Magic

Twitter offers insights into daily hubbub around the world with access to its constant feed. TweetBeat helps to sort through this massive amount of data to give a sense of the energy of a place.

Finding the Paths

A variety of keywords and timespans are used to figure it all out. We find words such as 'best' or 'fun' or 'music' for your city/swamp and show what they're associated with. We make counts of recurrent words in these contexts and organize these based on popularity and date. Find popular words, see where they take you.
